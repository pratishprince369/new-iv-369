Frequently Asked Questions

What is IV Therapy?

IV Therapy is a medical treatment that delivers fluids, vitamins, and minerals directly into the bloodstream for 100% absorption and immediate results.

Is Revive IV Hydration safe?

Yes! All treatments are administered by licensed Registered Nurses following strict medical protocols and oversight from our medical director.

How long does a session take?

A typical IV drip appointment takes between 45 to 60 minutes from start to finish, depending on the treatment type.

Do you take insurance?

We do not accept insurance; however, we can provide you with a superbill that you can submit to your insurance for potential reimbursement. We also accept HSA and FSA cards.

Where do you travel to?

We serve all of New York City (Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens), Long Island, The Hamptons, and parts of New Jersey and Connecticut Suburbs.
